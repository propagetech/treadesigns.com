ABOUT US

Our Story

TREA is a company working towards simplifying your software testing needs. We are a group of individuals who carry rich industry experience having worked at some of the esteemed organizations like, Microsoft, HP, Broadcom,...etc across various product domains. Our goal is to help you streamline your software testing process and help you at every stage of the software testing life cycle, right from defining testing processes, designing test environments, developing test automation frameworks, functional / non-functional test execution, continuous integration / continuous deployment (CI/CD) to collecting test metrices. We also provide web application solutions using PHP.

Less is more and simple yet powerful - It's easy to make things complicated, but it's very hard to make things that are easier to use. We strongly believe in this philosophy and what we strive for at TREA.
