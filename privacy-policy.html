PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated August 28, 2024
This Privacy Notice for TREA DESIGNS LLP ( " we ," " us ," or " our " ), describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ( " process " ) your personal information when you use our services ( " Services " ), including when you:
  • Download and use our mobile application ( anyone_there) , or any other application of ours that links to this Privacy Notice
  • Use anyone_there . Request to know the status (open or closed) or information about any place (stores, restaurants, temples, clinics, etc), anytime, anywhere with the "anyone_there?" app.

    Sometimes people can't find the status of a place online and sometimes there might not be a phone number available to get more information about a location. Well, not anymore. For example, the status of a store or a restaurant might be displayed as open online, but in reality, the restaurant might be closed. In cases like this, if someone wants to know if a store or a restaurant is actually open, or if they want to know if a particular item is available somewhere, all they have to do is just place a request on the anyone_there app providing sufficient details, and someone near the location can volunteer to help.

    Without making a trip, with the anyone_there app at your fingertips, it's now so easy to get any kind of information about a place. The app will completely bypass the hassle of you driving all the way to places where the commute might be impractical.

    Help others and get an opportunity to earn as well
    Using the anyone_there app, you can not only request to find out information about places, but also help others who need similar information about stores, restaurants, etc in your vicinity and you can earn money while doing so.

    Find out information from almost anywhere
    With the anyone_there app, you can find out information about any place from anywhere. It is not restricted to any region or geography.

    Nominal prices
    Placing requests on the anyone_there app is available at a nominal service fee, and you can decide what price to pay after discussing with the person who is providing you the information.

9. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES
Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and mobile applications include a Do-Not-Track ( "DNT" ) feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected. At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals has been finalized .
